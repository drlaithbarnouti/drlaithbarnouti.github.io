The Medical Career

A Legacy Grounded in Deep Surgical Competence

Dr Laith Barnouti (MBBS, FRACS Plast) serves as the primary medical mind and founder of **Sydney Plastic Surgery**. Over a distinguished career spanning **more than 20 years of active surgical operations**, Dr Barnouti has positioned himself as an authoritative figure in the complex fields of cosmetic, plastic, and reconstructive surgery within the Australian medical network. His practice architecture is designed from the ground up to prioritize absolute safety parameters, legal transparency, and unparalleled technical refinement.

Dr Barnouti's long journey into specialist medicine began with comprehensive clinical training layouts across multiple major tertiary teaching hospitals across Australia. His extensive operational history includes rigorous residencies and senior surgical roles at world-class institutions, including **St Vincent’s Hospital, Prince of Wales Hospital, Royal Prince Alfred Hospital, Royal Adelaide Hospital, and Westmead Hospital**. This wide exposure to complex trauma, soft-tissue reconstruction, and aesthetic transformations provided him with the profound deep-tissue fluency required to execute highly detailed procedures safely.

As an active holder of the **FRACS (Plast) fellowship**, Dr Barnouti belongs to the highly competitive, elite tier of medical practitioners recognized directly by the Australian Medical Council as fully qualified specialists. This specific distinction separates his practice profile from general cosmetic doctors, offering patients complete peace of mind that their treatment plans are governed by the highest ethical standards, continuous peer review frameworks, and deep anatomical comprehension.

Beyond the operating theatre, Dr Barnouti balances his clinical layout with important academic contributions. He serves as a **Conjoint Lecturer at the University of New South Wales (UNSW)**, sharing multi-decade insights and evidence-based surgical methodologies with senior medical students. He is also widely recognized as an accessible public commentator, appearing frequently on major national television structures including SBS, Channel 7, Channel 9, Channel 10, and specialized medical documentation programs like *Embarrassing Bodies Down Under*, where he offers impartial expert analyses regarding modern surgical protection rules.
