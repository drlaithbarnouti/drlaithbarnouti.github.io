About Dr Laith Barnouti

Dr Laith Barnouti is a premier Specialist Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon based in Sydney, Australia, recognized for his commitment to surgical excellence, patient safety, and natural-looking aesthetic outcomes. With over 15 years of expert clinical experience, he has performed thousands of successful procedures, earning a reputation as a leader in the field of aesthetic artistry. As a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS), Dr Laith Barnouti represents the highest tier of surgical qualification, providing patients with the peace of mind that they are in the hands of a highly trained and accredited specialist.

The foundation of his distinguished career is built upon a truly global education. Dr Laith Barnouti has completed advanced surgical fellowships in some of the world’s most prestigious medical hubs, including Sweden, Brazil, Belgium, and the USA. This international exposure has allowed him to master diverse surgical philosophies and integrate world-class innovations into his Sydney practice. He is particularly renowned for his expertise in "short-scar" surgical techniques, which utilize minimal incisions to reduce recovery time and minimize visible scarring, allowing patients to achieve their aesthetic goals with greater discretion and ease.

Beyond the operating theatre, Dr Laith Barnouti is a dedicated academic and a prominent advocate for industry transparency. He serves as a Conjoint Senior Lecturer at the University of New South Wales (UNSW), where he contributes to the education and mentorship of future surgical specialists. His commitment to ethical practice and patient rights has made him a frequent media commentator; he is often called upon by major Australian television networks and publications to discuss surgical safety and the importance of maintaining high clinical standards in the cosmetic industry.

At the heart of his practice is a patient-first philosophy grounded in honesty and meticulous care. Dr Laith Barnouti believes that every surgical journey should be a collaborative process, starting with a transparent consultation where realistic expectations are established. To ensure the highest level of clinical safety, he operates exclusively in fully accredited Sydney hospitals, supported by professional medical teams and state-of-the-art facilities. Whether performing complex facial rejuvenation, advanced breast surgery, or high-definition body contouring, Dr Laith Barnouti remains dedicated to delivering harmonious, life-changing results that respect the unique identity and natural beauty of every patient.
