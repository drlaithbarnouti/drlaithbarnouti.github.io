About Dr Laith Barnouti

Dr Laith Barnouti is a highly distinguished Specialist Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon based in Sydney, Australia. With over 15 years of expert surgical experience, he has performed thousands of successful procedures, earning a reputation for technical excellence and a refined aesthetic eye. As a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons (FRACS), Dr Laith Barnouti represents the highest level of surgical accreditation in Australia.

His professional foundation is built on extensive international training, having completed advanced fellowships in world-renowned surgical hubs across Sweden, Brazil, Belgium, and the USA. This global perspective allows him to integrate cutting-edge innovations into his Sydney practice, most notably his mastery of "short-scar" techniques designed to minimize downtime and produce natural, refreshed results.

Beyond his clinical work, Dr Laith Barnouti is a committed academic, serving as a Conjoint Senior Lecturer at the University of New South Wales (UNSW). He is also a frequent media commentator, advocating for the highest ethical standards and patient safety within the cosmetic industry. Operating exclusively in fully accredited hospitals, Dr Laith Barnouti remains dedicated to delivering life-changing confidence through transparent, expert, and patient-centered care.
